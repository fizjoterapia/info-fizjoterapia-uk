Sports Rehabilitation

About FizjoTerapia UK

Your trusted partner in physiotherapy care

Our Story

FizjoTerapia UK was established in 2015 by a team of Polish physiotherapists who saw the need for specialized physiotherapy services in the UK. With a combination of Polish physiotherapy techniques and UK healthcare standards, we've been providing exceptional care to our patients.

Our Mission

We are committed to improving the quality of life for our patients through personalized physiotherapy treatments. Our goal is to not only treat the symptoms but to address the root cause of the problem to ensure long-term health and well-being.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly qualified physiotherapists with diverse specializations. Each member brings unique expertise and experience, allowing us to provide comprehensive care for various conditions.
